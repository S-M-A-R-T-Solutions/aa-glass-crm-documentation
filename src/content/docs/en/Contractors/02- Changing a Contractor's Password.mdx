<Aside type='caution'>
    By default, Contractors are created with a temporary and unavailable password (created for security reasons). It's **Mandatory** change the password of a contractor for being able to login with it.
</Aside>

Changing a Contractor's Password is a simple process. Follow the steps below to change a Contractor's password.

![](/contractor/contractors-6.webp)

In Contractors Tab, click on the "lock" icon in the Contractor's row you want to change the password.

![](/contractor/contractors-7.webp)

In the "Change Password" dialog, enter the new password and confirm it. Click on the "Change Password" button.

<Aside type='note'>
    Due to Security reasons, all password must have at least 8 characters, including at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character.
</Aside>

Click on "Save" button to save the changes.

Congratulations! Now your contractor is able to login from to the CRM as a user.

Remember, the set of credential for login as a contractor is the username of the contactor (Check Contractor Details) and the password you just set.
